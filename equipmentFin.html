What is Equipment Financing?

Equipment financing refers to a loan used to purchase business-related equipment, such as a restaurant oven, a vehicle or a copier scanner. Equipment loans provide for periodic payments that include interest and principal over a fixed term. As security for the loan, the lender may require a lien on the equipment as collateral against your debt. Once the loan is paid in full, you own the equipment free of any lien.
All kinds of equipment are necessary for a multitude of businesses. Paying for it all at once just doesn't make sense for such a high expense. Instead, a Equipment Financing loan would allow you to pay it over time.
